Purity Calculator

In statistics class, I created a Purity Calculator which calculates the purity of child nodes using Gini Scores and Entropy. In order to use the calculator, you enter the number of objects of each type found in one node, then hit calculate.

Model of Median Income and Diversity

In statistics class, our final project we worked on a model to see if there was a correlation between diversity and the median income in a county. There was a correlation between skin color and median income in a county. Also as there were more women owned firms, the income went down. The model showed that there are many areas for improvement in diversity.

Quarterly Report

I have been working at Asset Liability Management for about 5 years now. One of my bigger projects has been automating the quarterly report. When I came on the job, the quarterly report was very manual and hard to understand. I redesigned the report and automated it. This brought the time spent on it from almost a month to a week.
