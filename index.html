About Me!

Hi I'm Angie! I am an Actuary Science Major and a Computer Science, Math and Math STAT minor, graduating in December of this year from St Thomas. My goal is to obtain a job in Data Analytics.

A bit about me, I was in the Marines as a Loadmaster on the C130. My job was to ensure the plane flew, by calculating load plans. The load plan was done on every flight to ensure the center of gravity on the plane was within a specific range.

As I did my job, I realized that I really loved math and looked for a civilian career where I could use as much math as possible. I'm excited to be so close to graduation and ready to start my career.
